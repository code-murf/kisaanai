## 🚀 The Vision
KisaanAI bridges the gap between advanced artificial intelligence and the daily challenges faced by Indian farmers. By providing a truly vernacular, voice-first, and highly intuitive platform, we deliver critical agricultural data—prices, weather, community insights, and crop disease diagnosis—directly to the farmer's hands.
